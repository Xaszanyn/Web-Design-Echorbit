About us

Echorbit Audio began its journey before the advent of corporatization in 2019. At that time, Echorbit Audio was primarily engaged in organizing sound design libraries for games and films, as well as providing post-production services. In 2023, the company officially came into being.

Echorbit Audio is a company that is growing and developing rapidly. It currently provides a wide range of services, including the production of sound design libraries for films and games, postproduction and music services, and sound implementation for games.

Echorbit Audio was founded with the aim of providing sound design and post-production services. In its early years, the company established its position in the industry by working with independent producers and small game studios. It quickly grew, forming partnerships with major film production companies and international game developers. Known for its innovative approach and customerfocused service, Echorbit Audio aims to add value to projects by delivering high-quality sound design and post-production solutions for the gaming and film industries.

With a clear vision to become a leading provider of sound design and post-production services. Starting with a small team, our company initially collaborated with independent filmmakers and emerging game studios. Over the years, through a commitment to quality and innovation, Echorbit Audio expanded its portfolio and began working with major film production houses and globally recognized game developers.

From the beginning, our focus has been on creating immersive and impactful soundscapes that enhance the storytelling and gameplay experience. We specialize in a range of services, including original music composition, sound design for games and films, and comprehensive post-production solutions such as ADR, sound mixing, and mastering.

Our studio is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, allowing us to deliver top-tier audio solutions that meet the evolving demands of the industry. We pride ourselves on staying at the forefront of sound technology, constantly investing in new tools and techniques to push the boundaries of what is possible in sound design.

At Echorbit Audio our mission is to collaborate closely with our clients, understanding their creative vision and translating it into sound. Whether it’s creating a haunting atmosphere for a horror film, designing dynamic sound effects for a blockbuster game, or crafting an original score that resonates with audiences, we are dedicated to bringing our clients’ projects to life through sound.

Our reputation for excellence has been built on years of delivering consistent, high-quality results, and we continue to strive for innovation and perfection in every project we undertake.

Our Mission

We provide game and film creators with sound design and post-production solutions that enhance the atmosphere and emotional depth of their projects, making every scene and every gaming moment unforgettable. Our mission is to transform our clients’ visions into reality by offering creative and technically flawless services tailored to their specific needs. We enrich our clients’ projects with highquality sounds that leave a lasting emotional impact on audiences and tell their stories more profoundly. As a team, we combine innovative technologies with our artistic vision, treating each project as a work of art and working with passion and precision to achieve excellence. Our goal is to establish long-term partnerships with our clients and be a reliable partner they can count on for every new project.

Our Vision

To redefine storytelling in the digital realms of the future through the power of sound. As a company, we aim to provide innovative sound design and post-production services that give each project a unique auditory identity in the game and film industries. Our vision is to add value to our clients’ creative ideas while becoming a globally recognized, trusted, and preferred partner in sound design and post-production. In the future, we aspire to continuously push the boundaries of sound, using all the possibilities that technology offers to deliver unforgettable experiences to audiences and players alike.
