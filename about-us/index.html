About us

Echorbit Audio was founded with a mission to elevate the sound design and post-production industries through innovative solutions and exceptional service quality. Since our establishment in 2019, we have been committed to providing comprehensive sound design libraries, post-production services, and music production solutions to clients in both the film and gaming industries. Our journey began with a small team working closely with independent filmmakers and game developers, and over time, we have expanded our scope to include partnerships with major film production houses and internationally recognized game studios.

Our company prides itself on staying at the forefront of sound technology and continuously investing in new tools and techniques. With a team of highly skilled sound designers, composers, and engineers, we offer a diverse range of services that bring stories to life and enhance the immersive quality of any project. At Echorbit Audio, we believe in the power of sound to transform narratives, and we are dedicated to crafting rich, dynamic soundscapes that resonate deeply with audiences.

With a clear vision to become a leading provider of sound design and post-production services, we work tirelessly to add value to every project we undertake, ensuring that our clients’ creative visions are realized in full. Our studio is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, allowing us to deliver top-tier audio solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each project.

Our services

Echorbit Audio offers a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of filmmakers, game developers, and other media professionals.

Film Sound Design & Audio Post Production

We specialize in crafting detailed and realistic audio landscapes that enhance the visual narrative of films. From ambient soundscapes that set the mood to dynamic sound effects that bring action scenes to life, our sound design services cover every aspect of audio post-production, including ADR, dialogue editing, Foley recording, sound mixing, and mastering.

Game Sound Design & Implementation

Our team is experienced in creating dynamic and interactive soundscapes for games. We design custom sound effects, ambient sounds, and ingame music that react to player actions and enrich the overall gaming experience. We also offer sound implementation services using middleware such as FMOD and Wwise to ensure seamless integration of our designs into your game engine.

Original Music Composition

Our composers specialize in creating original soundtracks that elevate the emotional impact of any visual or interactive medium. Whether it’s a sweeping orchestral score for an epic scene or a minimalist, tension-building theme for a suspenseful moment, we craft music that perfectly aligns with your project’s narrative and tone.

SFX Libraries

We produce high-quality, ready-to-use sound libraries for use in film, game, and multimedia projects. Our libraries are meticulously curated to provide sound designers with versatile assets that can be easily integrated into a variety of contexts, from trailers and scenes to Foley work.
